Support out cause

NGOs and CBOs in Trinidad & Tobago provide life changing services to sustain the social fabric of our country. With our economy at a crossroad between development and decline we are seeing rising unemployment, increasing crime and deepening poverty. This makes the role of NGOs/ CBOs more critical even though many organisations are suffering from reduced funding and are restrained by dwindling human resources in the pursuit of their goals.

The Volunteer Center of Trinidad & Tobago (VCTT) is currently working with a targeted set of NGOs/ CBOs whose projects align with the Sustainable Development Goals on a full spectrum volunteer matching system which includes:

  • Training of volunteers on the Sustainable Development Goals
  • Needs assessment and mapping with partners of all their volunteer needs
  • Strategic recruitment and connection of technical volunteers to areas of high impact within the organisation.

Throughout this process we work closely with partners to monitor and evaluate the impact of our engagements on their footprint and capacity development. We forecast that we will be able to extend a robust full spectrum volunteer matching service to a wider net of NGOs/ CBOs within the next year pending the learnings and outcome of the current engagement.

Your financial support is crucial to ensuring we achieve these goals.
