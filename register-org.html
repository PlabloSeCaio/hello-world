The Volunteer Center of Trinidad and Tobago (VCTT) has been a close ally since 2015, providing support for our work. This year, VCTT partnered with us on NEW FIRE Festival (NFF) our flagship initiative, as one of our 31 Official Collaborators. They provided Volunteer Coordination and Management Services to TTBI for the successful coordination of NFF 2017 which included, recruiting volunteers for the festival and related events, developing a volunteer schedule for the festival and managing volunteers at the festival.

NFF was successfully implemented this year due in no small part to the work of VCTT. Over 100 volunteers were recruited through their efforts and they capably managed these persons at the festival which included three days of festival activities. They also assisted in our monitoring and evaluation efforts ensuring that substantial data was captured that we will use to inform the planning and implementation of future editions of NFF. We look forward to continuing our relationship with VCTT as we harness the potential of our people. This organisation is well structured and continues to strive to develop their operational practices.

Elize Rostant
Director, Trinidad and Tobago Bridge Initiative
Festival Coordinator, NEW FIRE Festival
Designer and Culture Specialist
